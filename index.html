The Competitions

Two Battles, One Stage

🚁

AERO BLAZE

National Level Drone Race

Competition Rules

  • 01 Max drone weight: 1 kg including battery.
  • 02 Electric motor drones only.
  • 03 Only one pilot per drone on the track at a time.
  • 04 Maximum flying height allowed: 10 feet from ground level during the race.
  • 05 Complete the course by passing through all gates. The fastest time wins.

Cash Prizes

🥇 Winner ₹10,000
🥈 Runner Up ₹5,000
🤖

GROUND WARRIOR'S ARENA

National Level Robot Race

Competition Rules

  • 01 Wires should remain slack during the run. Pulling the wire to help the robot move or traverse the course may lead to disqualification.
  • 02 If any robot starts before the official start call, the timer will be restarted and the team will get a second chance. If the same mistake is repeated again, the team will be disqualified.
  • 03 Your robot must be ready when your team is called.

Cash Prizes

🥇 Winner ₹5,000
🥈 Runner Up ₹3,000
